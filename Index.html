The Moon

A Natural Satellite

The Moon is the only natural satellite of the Earth, and the fifth largest satellite in the
Solar System. It is the largest natural satellite of a planet in the Solar System relaive
to the size of its primary, having a quarter of the diameter of the Earth but only 1/81 its'
mass.
